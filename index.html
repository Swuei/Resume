About Me

Hey! I'm Swuei and I’ve been playing Minecraft inconsistently for around 11 years now. I am an experienced staff member with a wide variety of gamemode experience, having achieved ranks ranging from Helper to Admin over a span of 4 years.

I have been staffing on Minecraft servers for 3 to 4 years and have achieved many great things that have greatly benefited the servers I’ve had the privilege to be part of. My vision when staffing is to cultivate an enjoyable, fun environment for both players and staff—leaving a lasting positive impression.

Some notable aspects about me include my undying dedication to every project I’m part of, the ability to maneuver through obstacles, and swift decision-making to streamline support tickets. I have years of experience moderating and leading teams. I aim to master everything there is to know about Minecraft server staffing so I can be a reliable asset and adapt to any new challenges.

3D Modeling is a hobby that I got into and I have been doing it for a while now (not too long), I enjoy doing it since it's calming and I'm able to help server owners by giving them free models that they do not need to pay for so that they can instead use those funds on more important features for their server.
